About Freezehouse Berhampur

Founded with the vision to bring modern recovery science to Odisha, Freezehouse Berhampur is a pioneer in cryotherapy and ice bath services. We are committed to helping individuals achieve physical and mental resilience through the power of cold immersion therapy.

🌟 Our Mission

To promote wellness and recovery by offering safe, accessible, and effective ice bath experiences that help our clients rejuvenate, recover, and reset.

👥 Who We Serve

  • Athletes and fitness enthusiasts
  • Working professionals with stress or fatigue
  • Individuals managing pain, inflammation, or injury
  • Anyone seeking a physical and mental energy boost
